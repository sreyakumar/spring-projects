Let's make cake!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of Strawberries
  • Ready made cheesecake
  • Sugar to your liking

Instructions

  1. Defrost the cheesecake
  2. Heat up cut stawberries and sugar in a pan under low heat till jammy consistency forms
  3. Serve compote on top of cheesecake and enjoy!
